‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

üí∞ How much does it cost?
Most tools offer free credits to start (usually 100-1000 checks). After that, expect to pay ¬£0.005-¬£0.02 per number checked. For 10,000 numbers monthly, budget around ¬£50-200.
‚öñÔ∏è Is this legal in the UK?
Yes! Phone validation is legal and actually helps you stay compliant. Some tools automatically check against the TPS (Do Not Call) list, which is required by law before making marketing calls.
üöÄ How fast is it?
Single numbers: Instant results. Bulk uploads: Usually 1-5 minutes per 1000 numbers. Large files (100,000+) might take 30-60 minutes.
üéØ How accurate is it?
Good tools are 95-98% accurate. They can't be 100% perfect because phone numbers change ownership, get disconnected, or have network issues. But they'll catch most invalid numbers.
üì± Can I use it on my phone?
Yes! Most tools work in web browsers on phones and tablets. Some also have mobile apps. Perfect for sales reps checking numbers on the go.
üîÑ How often should I check?
New numbers: Check immediately. Existing database: Every 3-6 months. High-activity lists: Monthly. Phone numbers change ownership or get disconnected regularly.
